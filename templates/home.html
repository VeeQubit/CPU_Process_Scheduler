Supported Algorithms

Six Scheduling Algorithms

From simplest to most advanced — all implemented with full preemption support.

Non-Preemptive
FCFS
First Come First Serve

Executes processes in strict arrival order. Simple and fair but can cause the convoy effect with long jobs.

O(n log n)
Non-Preemptive
SPN
Shortest Process Next

Minimizes average waiting time by selecting the shortest burst process from the ready queue.

O(n²)
Preemptive ★
SRTF
Shortest Remaining Time First

Optimal average waiting time. Preempts the running process whenever a shorter job arrives.

O(n²)
Preemptive
RR
Round Robin

Time-sharing with configurable quantum. Provides fair CPU time distribution across all processes.

O(n)
Non-Preemptive
Pri·NP
Priority Non-Preemptive

Runs the highest-priority process to completion before scheduling the next one.

O(n²)
Preemptive
Pri·P
Priority Preemptive

Can interrupt the running process when a higher-priority job arrives in the queue.

O(n²)
